THE EVENT

Pragma is premier student developers’ conference held at IIIT-Allahabad. Attracting some of the best student developers from across India, Pragma is a 3 day extravaganza featuring a hackathon, workshops and numerous talks.Pragma’s main aim is to spark conversations between the students and get their ideas flowing because young blood often produces revolutionary ideas.

TALKS


Pragma will feature talks from some of the most experienced and sought after people from the developer community. Upto this point, you’ve only only read their blogs but now you’ll get a chance to hear them speak on our stage and interact with them personally!

WORKSHOPS


Pragma will also feature numerous workshops to provide hands-on experience of latest cutting edge technologies that would have been difficult for you to access otherwise. It will be a great learning experience and you may expect to leave with an idea of what technologies in the future will look like.

ABOUT IIIT ALLAHABAD


IIITA is one of the premier engineering colleges of India. It is recognised as an Institute of National Importance by the ​ Ministry of Human Resource Development​ . ​ Established in 1999, as a center of excellence in Information Technology and allied areas. Institute was conferred the Deemed University status by Govt. of India in the year 2000. Regarded to have one of the best coding cultures among colleges in India, IIIT-A is known for producing tech-savvy graduates every year.

GEEKHAVEN


GeekHaven, the Technical Society of IIIT-Allahabad, consists of many wings such as FOSS, Web Development, App Development, Design, Blockchain, Software Development, Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence and Competitive Coding.Comprising of some of the best technical minds of the college, GeekHaven is responsible for organising technical events throughout theyear and promoting an overall technical culture in the college by holding regular workshops and quick-talks.
